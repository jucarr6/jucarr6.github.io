Green Geeks

  • Green Geeks is the World's #1 Green Energy webhost that makes a positive impact on the environment starting at only $2.95 per month. Not only is Green Geeks recognized at a global level by the Environmental Protection Agency, but they also plant a tree for every hosting account provision on their platform, giving back one tree at a time to improve their carbon footprint.
